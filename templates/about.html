    Our Story

    LimeLine emerged from our passion for agriculture and our commitment to supporting farmers and gardeners in growing healthy, thriving calamansi plants. Aware of the challenges posed by plant diseases and their impact on crop yield and quality, we aimed to develop a solution that blends the latest technology with practical agricultural knowledge.

    Our vision at LimeLine is to lead the way in agricultural innovation, making advanced disease detection accessible to every farmer and gardener. We aspire to create a future where plant health is easily manageable, and crop losses due to disease are a thing of the past.

    We are dedicated to empowering our users with the tools and knowledge they need to maintain the health of their calamansi plants. By offering reliable, accurate, and easy-to-use solutions, we aim to reduce crop loss, improve yield, and contribute to the overall sustainability of the calamansi industry.

    Our Values

    Innovation

    We leverage cutting-edge technology to provide highly effective disease detection tools. Our advanced solutions are designed to offer support for managing calamansi crop health.

    Education

    We believe in the power of knowledge. Our platform not only detects diseases but also educates users on how to manage and prevent them.

    Sustainability

    We are committed to promoting sustainable practices for calamansi crops that benefit our users. Our focus is on ensuring the long-term health and productivity of these crops.

    Community Collaboration

    We have integrated a chat feature in CalamansiCare to enhance collaboration among farm owners and members, allowing them to share insights and work together for optimal calamansi plant health. This tool underscores the importance of effective communication in agricultural management.

    Meet Our Team

    LimeLine is a capstone project developed by a team of dedicated students passionate about agriculture and technology. As part of our academic journey, we have created this platform to address real-world challenges in calamansi disease detection. Through this project, we aim to apply our knowledge and skills to develop practical solutions that can make a difference in the field of plant health.
